# Number Wars
### The *Perfect* Time-Passer
Fight 'till wits end in **Number Wars**! <br>
Place down numbers against your opponent! <br>
Get points for **matching pairs**, **adding to ten**, or **make a sequence**! <br>
Becareful and make sure the paths match up though! If they don't meet then you won't get any points! <br>
Get **credits** after winning a round! <br>
Use those credits in the **store** or in the **casio** to make more! <br>
Use the items you got in the **store** in the **settings** and change up your game! <br>
